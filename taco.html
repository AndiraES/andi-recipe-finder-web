    About Taco :
    Taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or wheat tortilla filled with various ingredients such as grilled or stewed meats (like beef, pork, chicken, or fish), vegetables, cheese, salsa, guacamole, and sometimes beans or rice. It's often folded or rolled and eaten by hand, making it a versatile and beloved street food in Mexico and popular worldwide.

    Ingredients:

    • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
    • 1 lb (450g) of your choice of protein (e.g., beef, chicken, pork, or fish)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, diced
    • 1 cup shredded lettuce or cabbage
    • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • 1 lime, cut into wedges
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional toppings: salsa, sour cream, grated cheese
    • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
    • Handful of cilantro leaves (for garnish)
    Instructions:
    1. Prepare your choice of protein by seasoning it with salt and pepper. Cook it according to your preference (grill, sauté, or roast).
    2. Warm the tortillas by heating them in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or wrap them in foil and heat in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 5-10 minutes.
    3. Assemble your tacos: Place a spoonful of the cooked protein onto each tortilla. Top with chopped onion, diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce or cabbage, cilantro, and avocado slices.
    4. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos.
    5. Add any optional toppings you desire, such as salsa, sour cream, or grated cheese.
    6. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade tacos!

    Feel free to customize your tacos with your favorite ingredients and toppings to suit your taste preferences.
