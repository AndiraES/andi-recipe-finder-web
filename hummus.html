    About Hummus :
    Hummus is a popular Middle Eastern dip or spread made from cooked and mashed chickpeas, blended with tahini (sesame seed paste), lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil. It's typically seasoned with salt and other spices like cumin or paprika. Hummus is enjoyed throughout the Middle East and has gained popularity worldwide for its creamy texture and versatile flavor. It's often served as an appetizer with pita bread or raw vegetables, and it's a staple in many Middle Eastern cuisines.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas (also known as garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
    • 1/4 cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
    • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
    • 2-3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
    • Salt, to taste
    • Powdered sugar
    • Optional garnishes: paprika, chopped parsley, pine nuts
    Instructions:
    1. Prepare the Chickpeas :
      • Drain and rinse the chickpeas under cold water.
      • If you have time, you can remove the skins from the chickpeas by gently pinching them between your fingers. This step is optional but can result in a smoother hummus.
    2. Blend the Ingredients :
      • In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, and a pinch of salt.
      • Process the mixture until smooth and creamy. If the hummus is too thick, you can add a tablespoon or two of water to reach your desired consistency.
    3. Adjust Seasoning :
      • Taste the hummus and adjust the seasoning as needed. Add more salt, lemon juice, or garlic to suit your taste preferences.
    4. Serve :
      • Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl.
      • Drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil and sprinkle with optional garnishes such as paprika, chopped parsley, or pine nuts.
      • Serve the hummus with pita bread, crackers, raw vegetables, or as a spread on sandwiches or wraps.

    Enjoy your homemade hummus! It's a delicious and healthy snack or appetizer that's perfect for sharing with friends and family.
